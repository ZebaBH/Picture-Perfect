ABOUT THE PROJECT

Picture Perfect is an interactive disposable camera experience that invites participants to challenge the idea of perfection. By taking a photo and drawing or writing over it, users transform their image into something personal, imperfect, and uniquely their own. The project encourages reflection on how perfectionism can shape the way we see ourselves and celebrates the beauty found in creative self-expression.

WHY PARTICIPATION MATTERS

Every mark tells a story. By altering your photo, you actively disrupt the pressure to appear flawless and embrace the imperfections that make us human. Through participation, the project creates a space for reflection, helping people recognize that growth, creativity, and self-worth do not come from being perfect, but from being authentic.
